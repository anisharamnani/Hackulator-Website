Learn about how awesome we are:

Avi Karnani developed a lifelong love of learning and math when in montessori school his teacher thought it better to let him play with a calculator instead of naptime. Avi later saw that students in under resourced schools didn't have access to graphing calculators; they were locked up and only available for short segments of classroom time.

Anisha Ramnani thinks math is awesome and wants people to love math. She feels too many students are overwhelmed by the fact that math builds on previously learned material over the years and doesn't want it to be that way. She’s going to make sure this calculator helps people be less scared of math.
Oliver Switzer, a lifelong tinkerer who in college decided Physics is where it’s at. He spent more time working with graphing calculators than a healthy person should; like a loving partner he understands their flaws and wants to help them be better.
